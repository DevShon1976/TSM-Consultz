How It Works

From Upload to Placement Decision in 30 Minutes.

The TSM simulation engine puts candidates through real job scenarios — not quizzes — and returns actionable workforce intelligence, not just a number.

01

Role Selection

Candidate or recruiter selects the target role from TSM's sector library — Medical Billing, AR Specialist, AP Analyst, Claims Rep, Accountant, or Bookkeeper.

02

Live Workflow Scenario

Candidate receives a real-world scenario — denied Medicare claim, AP reconciliation mismatch, prior auth appeal — built from TSM's deployed case library.

03

AI Evaluation Engine

TSM's Groq-powered AI scores correctness of decision path, workflow logic sequence, time-to-resolution, and compliance adherence in real time.

04

Scorecard Output

Agency receives a full placement scorecard: readiness score 0–100, risk classification, role-fit prediction, strengths, risks, and a binary placement recommendation.
