// AI ENGINE

The 4-Node Pipeline

Every module click triggers all four AI nodes in sequence. Each builds on the previous. Full executive analysis in under 30 seconds.

APP 01
BNCA NODES
INTEL · TRIAGE · FLAGS · RISK ID
Document triage and risk identification. Lists every risk, anomaly, violation, or missing item with specific amounts, dates, and names. Outputs financial exposure total and 48-hour action items.
APP 02
HC STRATEGIST
RFI · ALERTS · COSTS · PRIORITIES
Operational strategy layer. Identifies RFI and change order impacts, cost alert line items, vendor/subcontractor issues, schedule re-sequencing needs, and 3 ranked mitigation options with cost/time comparison.
APP 03
MAIN STRATEGIST
LETTERS · MITIGATION · ACTIONS · SCHEDULE
Formal correspondence and mitigation planning. Drafts professional notice letters, step-by-step mitigation plans with owners and deadlines, schedule impact analysis on critical path, and legal/contractual considerations.
APP 04
EXECUTIVE PORTAL
CFO · REVENUE · ROADMAP · DECISIONS
Owner and CFO-level brief. 3-sentence executive summary, financial dashboard with key numbers, specific yes/no decisions required with deadlines, cash flow and retainage impact, and 30/60/90 day forward roadmap.
How to use the pipeline tabs: After a module runs, use the 01 BNCA 02 STRATEGIST 03 MAIN STRAT 04 EXECUTIVE tabs in the Intelligence Feed to switch between all four outputs. Each tab can be saved individually to the Report Builder using the SAVE → button.

// GETTING STARTED

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Six steps from opening the suite to sending an executive report. Follow in order your first time.

01
Open AuditOps Pro — Your Daily Command Center
▶

AuditOps Pro is your primary interface. Open it every morning. The left sidebar shows your active playbook and system status. The center grid shows all available modules organized by category. The right panel is your Report History and Custom Report Builder.

🔒tsm-shell.fly.dev/construction-suite/construction-pro.html
LIVE
▶ LAUNCH AUDITOPS PRO
First time? The Neural Core Key field at the top of the Intelligence Feed is hidden — AI runs server-side automatically. Just click any module card to start.
02
Select a Playbook from the Left Sidebar
▶

The left sidebar lists your active playbooks — preset scenario contexts like Material Theft, Safety Protocol, OSHA Readiness Audit, Lien Exposure Review, Cash Flow Stress Test, and Bid Strategy Analysis. Selecting a playbook sets the context for the pipeline — all 4 nodes will frame their analysis around that scenario.

Pro tip: Match your playbook to your current P1 issue. If you have a lien dispute active, select Lien Exposure Review before clicking any module card — the pipeline will tailor all 4 outputs to that context.
03
Click a Module Card — Pipeline Fires Automatically
▶

Click any module card in the center grid — OSHA Compliance Audit, Cost Overrun Recovery, Lien Exposure Review, etc. The 4-node pipeline fires immediately. Watch the node indicators progress: BNCA → HC Strategist → Main Strategist → Executive Portal. Each completes in sequence, streaming output directly into the Intelligence Feed tabs.

Tab switching: While the pipeline runs, click the 01 BNCA / 02 STRATEGIST / 03 MAIN STRAT / 04 EXECUTIVE tabs to read each node's output as it completes — you don't have to wait for all 4 to finish.
04
Use the Document Showcase for File-Based Analysis
▶

For document-specific analysis — timesheets, lien waivers, GL extracts, subcontractor invoices, OSHA citations — use the BNCA Neural Bridge. Drop any file (PDF, CSV, XLSX, image scan) or paste raw text. Select your document type from the left panel, then click Fire All 4 Apps.

🔒tsm-shell.fly.dev/construction-suite/document-showcase.html
LIVE
▶ OPEN NEURAL BRIDGE
Supported formats: PDFCSVXLSXJPG/PNG scansPlain text paste — or use the 5 preloaded sample documents: Fastener Delay, GL Extract Q1, Lien Waiver, Labor Timesheet, HOME Expenditure.
05
Save Outputs and Build Your Custom Report
▶

After any pipeline run, click SAVE → in the Intelligence Feed to add that output to your Report History. The right panel tracks all saved outputs with timestamps. Use the Custom Report Builder input to name your report, then click + Add All Saved to compile everything into one document. Export as .TXT or copy to clipboard.

Report flow: Run 3–4 modules throughout the day → Save each output → At EOD, compile into Custom AuditOps Report → Export .TXT → Send to superintendent / owner / CFO.
06
Distribute the Executive Portal Output
▶

The Executive Portal (App 04) output is designed for owner and CFO audiences — no jargon, numbers front and center, specific decisions required. Copy the Executive Portal tab output from any module run and include it in your daily owner report, board package, or pre-construction meeting brief.

What's in the Executive Portal output: Executive Summary (3 sentences) · Financial Dashboard (exposure, cash impact, mitigation cost) · Owner Decisions Required (with deadlines) · Revenue/Cash Flow Impact · 30/60/90 Day Roadmap.
