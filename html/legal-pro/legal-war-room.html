LEGAL WAR ROOM · HOW-TO GUIDE
► WHAT IS THE LEGAL WAR ROOM?

Welcome to the Legal War Room — TSM's 6-engine legal document analysis pipeline. Processes Contracts, MSAs, NDAs, Litigation Filings, Demand Letters, Regulatory Notices, eDiscovery Packages, Settlement Agreements, Compliance Audits.

► ENGINE PIPELINE
ENGINE 01
Extracts parties, effective dates, obligations, termination clauses, penalty triggers, and jurisdiction indicators from the raw legal document.
ENGINE 02
Identifies contract breaches, statute of limitations risks, unfavorable indemnification clauses, and litigation exposure flags.
ENGINE 03
Calculates total legal exposure, potential damages, penalty amounts, and settlement value range in dollars.
ENGINE 04
Audits regulatory compliance, GDPR/HIPAA/SOX obligations, filing deadlines, and jurisdictional exposure.
ENGINE 05
Synthesizes Engines 01–04 into a dollar-quantified risk mitigation plan with counsel assignments and response deadlines.
ENGINE 06 / NODES
Routes findings to the highest-priority TSM Legal apps with tailored how-to instructions for each node.
► ESCALATION CHAIN

After analysis, escalate to the Legal Chief Strategist via the escalation button. Use EXPORT to download a full plaintext report for counsel review.

PRO TIPS
▸ Load an MSA sample → fire engines → read Engine 02 for unfavorable indemnification flags
▸ Engine 03 calculates settlement value range — use before entering mediation
▸ Engine 04 flags HIPAA/GDPR obligations before escalating to Chief Strategist
▸ Anomaly Advisor auto-launches after engines — review field-level defects before escalating
