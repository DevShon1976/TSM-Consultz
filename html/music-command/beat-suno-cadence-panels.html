CADENCE TOOLS GUIDE
How to use Tap Tempo, Cadence Guide & Speak Cues
▼
Cadence is the rhythmic flow and timing of your vocal delivery — how your bars land on the beat. The cadence tools help you feel the pocket before you record so your bars hit exactly where they should.
👆
TAP TEMPO
Tap this button in rhythm with your beat — like tapping your foot. After 3–4 taps it calculates your exact BPM. All producer notes and cadence playback sync to this tempo.
💡 Pull up your beat on Spotify or YouTube, play it, and tap along. Now your session is locked to your actual beat.
🥁
PLAY CADENCE GUIDE
Plays a metronome click pattern at your tapped BPM directly in your browser — no app needed. Beat 1 = sharp high click (punch here), Beat 3 = medium click (accent), Beats 2 & 4 = soft click (ghost).
💡 Use this while reading your lyrics aloud to practice your flow BEFORE recording. Find where your bars naturally land.
🗣️
SPEAK CADENCE CUES
Uses your browser's text-to-speech to read ZAY's delivery instructions out loud. So instead of reading "punch beat 1" — you hear it while you practice. Hands-free coaching.
💡 Click this, then immediately start reading your hook out loud. The cues play in your ear as you rap.
Recommended Workflow
01 Fill in your concept (genre, mood, theme) and click Build My Song Blueprint
02 Click Generate Beat Prompt → copy → paste into Suno or Udio → generate your beat
03 Paste your beat link into the Beat Player — now it plays while you work
04 Tap Tap Tempo along with your beat to lock your BPM
05 Click Play Cadence Guide → practice your hook flow to the click before recording
06 Click Speak Cadence Cues while rapping your lines — ZAY coaches your delivery hands-free
07 Record. You know your pocket. Hit record.
Note: The Cadence Guide and Speak Cues use your browser's built-in audio — no plugins needed. Make sure your browser volume is up. On mobile, tap the screen first to unlock audio (browsers require a user interaction before playing sound).
