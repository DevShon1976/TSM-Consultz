PRESALES Interview Story Bank — TSM Shell → AB-100 Domain Mapped USE THESE IN EVERY SALESFORCE / MICROSOFT / SERVICENOW INTERVIEW
How to Use This
Every presales interview has 5 question types. Each one maps to an AB-100 domain. Your answer is always: TSM Shell story → Microsoft vocabulary → ROI. Hover each question to see the answer framework.
"Tell me about a time you architected a multi-agent AI solution for an enterprise problem." DESIGN DOMAIN
→ "I architected a 4-stage agentic AI chain for supply chain crisis management — Understand (Situation Room) → Decide (Strategist V2 with 3 parallel scenario models) → Execute (Executive Portal with live tracking) → Trust (Explainability layer with confidence scoring and reasoning chains). Each stage is a discrete agent passing structured context to the next via a relay bus — the same A2A pattern Microsoft Foundry uses. The chain reduced decision time from days to under 30 minutes for an $8.2M exposure scenario."
"How do you ensure AI recommendations are trustworthy and auditable for enterprise clients?" RESPONSIBLE AI
→ "We built an explainability contract into every vertical — every AI recommendation surfaces a confidence score, evidence sources, reasoning chain, and impact delta before a human sees it. Nothing executes without explicit HITL approval in the Executive Portal — named, timestamped, auditable. That's Microsoft's Responsible AI Standard applied in production: transparency, accountability, and human oversight baked into the architecture, not bolted on."
"Walk me through how you'd assess whether agentic AI is the right solution for a client's problem." PLAN DOMAIN
→ "I start with three questions: Is the decision repeatable enough to encode? Does it require context from multiple sources? And is there a human in the loop who can validate before action? If yes to all three, agentic AI fits. I then quantify the ROI: in healthcare denial management, that's $48K in stalled revenue recoverable in 90 days. In supply chain, it's a 6-hour decision window on $8.2M exposure. The ROI model goes into the client brief before we design anything."
"How have you deployed AI governance and compliance in a production environment?" DEPLOY DOMAIN
→ "Our platform ships with governance as architecture: HITL approval at every execution stage, session-token auth via HttpOnly cookies, HMAC-signed tokens, client-isolated data stores, and a full explainability audit trail per decision. We also built a Collective Intelligence layer that aggregates signals across all 7 verticals in real time — so leadership sees platform-wide risk posture, not just vertical-specific alerts. That's the monitoring and telemetry layer Microsoft requires for responsible agentic deployment."
"What's your process for translating a client's business problem into an AI solution design?" DESIGN + PRESALES
→ "I run a 3-step discovery: first, identify the decision bottleneck — where does a human spend time analyzing before acting? Second, map the data sources that inform that decision. Third, design the agent chain: intake → classify → analyze → recommend → approve → execute. I've done this across 7 verticals — the pattern is the same, the domain knowledge changes. I can demo a live version of this process in any of those verticals in under 20 minutes."
