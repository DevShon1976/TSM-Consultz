► WHAT IS THE RE WAR ROOM?

The RE War Room is TSM's Real Estate and Mortgage Intelligence Platform. It provides a 7-node analysis architecture, a Deal Rescue Pack for at-risk transactions, and a full escalation chain to the RE Strategist and Executive Portal. It handles: REA (Acquisition), REAF (Finance), REAT (Transaction), REAM (Mortgage Ops), REAC (Compliance), REAI (Market Intel), REAX (Closing).

► THE 7 NODES
REA · ACQUISITION
Buyer/seller intake, lead qualification, property matching, investment criteria, 70% rule analysis.
REAF · FINANCE
DTI calculation, loan product comparison (FHA/VA/Conventional/DSCR), pre-approval scoring, loan officer guidance.
REAT · TRANSACTION
Offer management, contract review, appraisal gap resolution, inspection negotiation, critical path to close.
REAM · MORTGAGE OPS
Underwriting conditions tracking, pipeline velocity, pull-through optimization, CTC projection, buyback risk.
REAC · COMPLIANCE
TRID audit, RESPA review, FinCEN RRE reporting, AML checklist, Fair Housing, HMDA for transactions over $300K.
REAI · MARKET INTEL
BPO methodology, comps, Phoenix metro trends, DOM, inventory levels, investment thesis, valuation analysis.
REAX · CLOSING
Title coordination, escrow coordination, HOA docs, CD delivery timing, funding readiness, closing delay rescue.
► DEAL RESCUE PACK

Click the ⚡ DEAL RESCUE PACK tab. Fill in the deal address, contract price, loan type, and closing date. Select all rescue scenarios that apply:

💸 Financing Failure — DTI, missing docs, asset sourcing
📉 Appraisal Gap — value below contract, comps needed
⚖️ Title Defect — liens, clouds, chain gaps
🔧 Inspection Issues — repairs, credits, negotiations
📋 UW Conditions — open conditions, CTC blocked
🚨 Closing Delay — HOA, title, insurance, wire
► ESCALATION CHAIN

After analysis: RE War Room → RE Strategist → RE Exec Portal. Each step receives the full prior session via localStorage relay. Click ESCALATE → STRATEGIST in the topbar to advance the chain.
