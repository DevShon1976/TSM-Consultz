Getting Started
WIA Platform Overview
WIA — Workforce Intelligence & Automation — is a unified operational platform with two deployment modes: The Placement Academy for staffing agencies and The Shift-Ready Bridge for healthcare enterprises.
What This Platform Does
◈ Staffing Mode
Moves candidates from raw applicant to interview-ready in under 72 hours. Tracks readiness scores, flags ghost risk, maintains a filtered ready-to-submit roster, and generates placement reports.
◉ Healthcare Mode
Bridges the gap between credentialing approval and floor deployment. Tracks primary source verification, OIG flags, unit orientation checklists, and Joint Commission compliance logs.
Tenant Switching
Switch between modes using the toggle in the top-right corner. All views, sidebar items, and data update instantly.
TSM AI Widget
The teal bar at the bottom-right is always available. Click it to open the AI command panel and ask any question about your workforce data or workflows.
◈
Use the left navigation to jump directly to any section. Each section explains what the view shows, how to use it, and what actions you can take.
Getting Started
Tenant Selection
When you first open WIA, you land on the Tenant Gate — a selection screen asking which mode you need.
How to Select Your Tenant
1
Read the two cards
The Placement Academy is for staffing agency branch operations. The Shift-Ready Bridge is for healthcare enterprise onboarding.
2
Click your mode
Click the card that matches your role. The platform loads with the correct sidebar, views, and data.
3
Switch modes any time
The ◈ Staffing / ◉ Healthcare toggle in the top-right lets you switch modes without returning to the gate.
◈
Force a specific mode on load by adding ?tenant=staffing or ?tenant=health to the URL.

Navigation & Layout
WIA has a consistent three-zone layout: the top bar, left sidebar, and main content area.
1
Top Bar
Shows the current mode name on the left. Right side has the tenant switcher, live status pill, and this How-To Guide button.

Staffing Mode
Branch Overview
Your daily command center. Opens by default when you enter Staffing mode. Shows real-time pipeline health and surfaces urgent items requiring recruiter attention.
Stat Cards
Active Applicants
Total candidates in your pipeline across all stages. Delta shows change since last week.
Ready to Submit
Candidates who passed every onboarding metric and are cleared for client submission. Your daily deliverable number.
Ghost Risk
Candidates flagged as unresponsive past the 12-hour threshold. Red = urgent intervention needed.
Avg Readiness Score
Composite readiness index across your active pipeline. Trend delta shows improvement vs last cycle.
1
Branch Pulse Table
Shows the 5 most recent candidates with track, readiness score bar, last-active timestamp, and status badge. Green = strong, amber = borderline, red = at risk.

Lower Panels

Staffing Mode
Ready-to-Submit Roster
Shows only candidates who have cleared every metric. Your daily submission queue — no filtering required.
1
Review the roster
Every candidate shown has passed readiness scoring, workflow accuracy, and compliance thresholds. Green border confirms this is a filtered, cleared list.
2
Check scores
Readiness % as a number. Workflow and Compliance as score bars. Rank candidates based on client requirements.
3
Submit to client

Predictive drop-off monitor. Flags candidates who have gone silent past defined time thresholds.

Red border. Immediate SMS outreach recommended. Pipeline loss likely without intervention within 2 hours.
Medium Risk (6–12h)
Amber border. Proactive ping now prevents escalation. Usually a stage-stall rather than true disengagement.
1
SMS Ping (individual)

2
Trigger SMS Blast
The red Trigger SMS Blast button sends a re-engagement message to all high-risk candidates simultaneously.

Healthcare Mode
System Overview
The healthcare operations command center. Real-time snapshot of staff pipeline health, verification status, compliance scores, and unit orientation progress.
Staff in Pipeline
Total clinical and non-clinical staff in the onboarding and verification process across all facilities.
PSV Verified
Staff who completed Primary Source Verification — license confirmed, OIG cleared, background complete. Your deployable pool.
OIG Flags
Staff with active OIG exclusion flags. No flagged staff can be scheduled until resolved.
Avg Time to Floor
Average days from credential submission to floor-ready status. Negative delta shows improvement vs standard hospital onboarding.
1
Staff Readiness Table
Each row: name, unit, license, OIG badge, background badge, PSV badge. Any non-green badge requires action before scheduling.
2
Lower Panels
Facility Compliance Summary shows score per facility. Unit Orientation Status shows fraction of staff completed per unit.
Healthcare Mode
PSV Tracker
Primary Source Verification tracking table. The most compliance-critical view in the platform.
1
State License
Verification via the issuing state licensing board — not self-reported. Shows CLEAR when verified.
2
OIG Check
FLAGGED = individual is on the OIG exclusion list. Cannot be employed in any federally-funded healthcare program until resolved. Immediate scheduling hold.
3
PSV Status
READY = all checks clear. REVIEW = checks in progress. HOLD = OIG flag — cannot proceed.
4
Run Batch Verify
Initiates a batch re-check of all pending PSV items simultaneously. Use at the start of each shift or after a bulk onboarding event.
◉
Any OIG flag automatically sets PSV to "Hold" — non-overridable. Resolve the OIG issue before any other steps.
Healthcare Mode
Compliance Audit Log
An unalterable, timestamped record of every credentialing, verification, and training event. Built to Joint Commission documentation standards.
1
Columns
Time, Event description, Actor (System or Admin name), and Date. System events are automated; Admin events are human-initiated.
2
Export Audit PDF
Generates a formatted, dated compliance report suitable for Joint Commission or CMS surveyors. All entries included with full actor attribution and timestamps.
◉
Log entries cannot be edited or deleted — by design for regulatory compliance. Export before audit visits for a static record at that point in time.
Healthcare Mode
Unit Orientation
Floor-specific onboarding checklists by unit (ICU, Med-Surg, ER, etc.). Ensures staff complete unit-specific protocols before their first scheduled shift.
1
Unit cards
Each unit shows name, assigned staff, and completion percentage. Below the header is the checklist of orientation items specific to that unit.
2
Checklist items
Green checkmark + strikethrough = completed. Empty checkbox = outstanding. Outstanding items block floor-ready status for that staff member in that unit.
◉
Staff floating between units must complete the orientation checklist for each unit independently. Completion does not transfer between units.
Healthcare Mode
Staff Readiness
Full clinical and non-clinical staff readiness table. Combines competency score, orientation progress, and PSV status into a single floor-ready determination.
1
Competency Score
Performance across competency blueprints for that staff member's system (Cerner, AppCentral, etc.).
2
Orientation %
Percentage of unit-specific orientation items completed. Must reach 100% for full floor clearance in most facilities.
3
Floor Ready
READY requires: competency ≥80%, orientation ≥80%, PSV = Verified. Any shortfall shows REVIEW or HOLD. Schedulers use this column to confirm eligibility before assigning a shift.
Healthcare Mode
Competency Blueprints
System-specific training modules tailored to the EMR and workflow systems used by each facility.
1
Banner Health — Oracle Cerner
PowerChart navigation, order entry and medication reconciliation, patient ADT workflow, clinical documentation standards, imaging and results review.
2
HonorHealth — AppCentral
Credential entry and validation, HonorHealth policy attestation, unit float pool orientation, incident reporting walkthrough, patient satisfaction protocol.
3
Completion rates
Each module shows "X/Y staff" completion. Low completion identifies a targeted training gap — schedule that session before the next deployment wave.
Healthcare Mode
Multi-Facility RBAC
Role-Based Access Control management. Controls which administrators can see data for which facilities.
1
Role hierarchy
Regional Admin = sees all facilities in region. Facility Admin = assigned facility only. Credential Manager = PSV and compliance data only. Unit Coordinator = orientation and scheduling data only.
2
Manage button
Click Manage ↗ on any facility to open access control settings — add or remove admins, adjust role assignments, audit recent access events.
Reference
Badge Reference
Every status badge in WIA follows a consistent color and meaning system.
READYAll required checks complete. Safe to proceed — submit, schedule, or deploy.
REVIEWBorderline or incomplete. Requires human judgment before next action.
GHOST RISKStaffing: candidate unresponsive 12h+. Healthcare: OIG flag or hold status.
PENDINGCheck initiated but not yet returned. Monitor — no action required yet.
CLEARSpecific check (OIG, background) passed cleanly. Component-level confirmation.
FLAGGEDSpecific check failed or returned a disqualifying result. Immediate review required.

Power User Tips
Staffing Mode
1
Daily recruiter routine (5 min)
Branch Overview → note Ready count → Ghost Risk → SMS Ping high-risk → Ready Roster → submit to open reqs. Done without manual candidate calls.
2
Force a mode via URL
Bookmark wia2.html?tenant=staffing or wia2.html?tenant=health to skip the gate every time.
3
Read the funnel weekly
Branch Analytics every Monday morning. The biggest stage drop tells you exactly where to focus training or sourcing changes for the week.
Healthcare Mode
4
Pre-audit checklist
Compliance Log → Export Audit PDF. PSV Tracker → confirm zero HOLD statuses. Unit Orientation → confirm all units at 100%. These three cover the majority of surveyor documentation requests.
5
OIG flag protocol
1) Do not schedule. 2) Confirm flag is timestamped in Compliance Log. 3) Contact staff for documentation. 4) Re-run batch verify. 5) Confirm PSV updates to READY before any scheduling.
6
Ask the AI
The TSM AI widget knows the platform. Ask: "Which staff are at risk of missing their floor date?" or "Walk me through the Ghost Risk tracker."
