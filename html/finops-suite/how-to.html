LANE 03
1099 / Tax
Filing deadlines, vendor thresholds, W-9 readiness, 1099-NEC vs MISC mapping, penalty exposure.
LANE 04
Bank Reconciliation
Strongest Close Loop · discrepancy detection, deposit-in-transit clearing, outstanding check tracing.
LANE 05
Month-End Close
All six lanes simultaneously · ranked action list, accrual posting, close checklist progression.
LANE 06
Compliance Audit
Documentation gaps, approval risk, audit trail validation, policy exception handling.
Pro tip: Paste multiple invoice lines or a CSV-style aging table directly into the Copilot context box. The AI extracts vendor names, amounts, and aging buckets automatically — no formatting required.
