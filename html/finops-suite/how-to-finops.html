TSM FinOps Suite · How To Guide
Everything you need to know.
All 8 apps. One guide.
The TSM FinOps Suite is your AI-powered accounting operations platform. It replaces manual AP/AR tracking, reconciliation, tax readiness checks, compliance monitoring, and financial reporting with real-time AI intelligence and one-click action plans.
The 8 Apps — Click to Learn
How the Suite Fits Together
1
Start at the Hub — financial-ui.html
The hub is your command center. All 8 apps are one click away. The Staff Accountant Guide panel on the right tells you what to click based on what you're trying to do.
2
Open the right app for your task
Each card is purpose-built. Processing invoices? Card 01. Something looks off in the financials? Card 02. Need to close the books? Card 03. Got a document to drop in? Card 08.
3
Run the AI and read the output
Every app is wired to the Groq AI backend (Llama 3.3 70B). Click the action button, wait 5–15 seconds, and get a specific, actionable response — not generic advice.
4
Export, copy, or push to Strategist
Every output can be copied, exported as a .txt file, or pushed to the FinOps Strategist for cross-app synthesis and owner lanes.

App 03 · Take Action
Controller Action Plan
Turns your accounting work into executive action. Prioritized next steps, outcome summary, and controller-ready recommendations. This is the "what do I do now" engine — it synthesizes all 5 other nodes and tells you exactly what to fix first.

⚡

2

1
Open Zero Trust (Card 06 on Hub)
Click "Open Zero Trust →" Run the access review first. Check for any users with more access than their role requires.
2
Review QuickBooks user list
The QB exposure panel is the most critical section. Admin access should be limited to 1–2 people maximum. Anything else is a risk.
TIP: Run this quarterly or any time someone leaves the organization. Departing employees are the #1 source of financial data breaches.
3
Generate the remediation report
The AI generates a step-by-step access remediation plan. Use it to revoke, downgrade, or review flagged permissions.

Pro Tips · Power User Guide
Get the Most Out of TSM FinOps
Tips from the team on getting maximum value from every app in the suite.
General Tips
★
Always start with App 01 in the morning
The daily dashboard sets your priorities for the day. Don't skip it — it saves you from chasing the wrong thing all morning.
★
Use App 08 for every new document that comes in
Any invoice, report, bank statement, or contract — drop it in App 08 first. 4 engines in 30 seconds beats 2 hours of manual review.
★
The Staff Accountant Guide on the hub is your shortcut menu
Don't memorize which app does what — just use the right panel on the hub. "Process AP/vendor invoices" → App 08. "Check audit support" → App 05. It maps everything for you.
★
Use the quick prompts in App 05 for compliance
The 6 pre-built quick prompts in Compliance Shield cover 90% of what you need. Click one, read the output, act on it. Don't type from scratch.
★
Export Engine 3 + 4 after every major document analysis
Manager Export gives you a ready-to-share file with the Controller Action Plan and CFO Intel. Send it instead of writing a summary yourself.
★
Run App 04 (Tax Prep) monthly Oct–Jan, not just in January
Catching a missing W-9 in October takes 30 seconds. Catching it on January 30 costs you a filing extension and possible penalties.
★
Use App 06 (Zero Trust) every time someone leaves the organization
Departing employees with active QuickBooks or financial system access are a significant risk. Run Zero Trust the day someone gives notice.
