⚡ HOW TO USE — FINOPS WAR ROOM

STEP 1 — LOAD YOUR DOCUMENT

  • Drop a financial document — P&L, invoice, AR aging, audit finding, or budget variance — into the intake zone or paste raw text.
  • Use a sample document to demo the pipeline instantly.

STEP 2 — CONFIGURE API KEY

  • Enter your Groq API key and hit Test. Green dot = connected. Saved to localStorage.

STEP 3 — FIRE ALL 6 ENGINES

  • E1 · DOCUMENT INTEL — Extracts entities, amounts, dates, account codes, document type.
  • E2 · RISK CLASSIFIER — Flags compliance gaps, audit exposure, cash flow risk signals.
  • E3 · FINANCIAL IMPACT — Quantifies dollar exposure, budget variance, collection risk.
  • E4 · RECOVERY STRATEGY — Action plan: collections, write-offs, escalation path.
  • E5 · ANOMALY ADVISOR — Detects outliers, duplicate charges, fraud signals.
  • E6 · NAVIGATOR — Routes tasks to Controller, AR Specialist, AP Coordinator, or CFO.

STEP 4 — DOWNSTREAM WORKFLOW

  • Output relays via sessionStorage to the FinOps Strategist and Executive Portal.
  • Use the Ecosystem nav to escalate to other TSM suites.
