🎓 Study Mode — How To Use
A complete self-guided learning environment for Staff Accountants preparing for FinOps technical assessments.
Use the modes below to master every concept before or after your interview session.
📚 Available Study Modes
🃏 Flashcards
Flip through 20 question/answer cards covering every demo topic. Click the card to reveal the answer. Use ← → arrows to navigate. Cards track your review progress automatically.
🧠 Quiz Mode
Multiple-choice quizzes with 4-option questions drawn from all 4 assessment categories. Each question includes a full explanation after you answer — great for understanding the "why," not just the "what."
📒 GL Reference
Quick-reference chart of all GL accounts used in the demos. Shows account number, name, type (Asset/Liability/Expense), and the debit/credit rule. Filter by account type.
✏️ JE Builder
Practice building balanced journal entries. Add debit and credit lines, select GL accounts, enter amounts. The balance checker confirms your entry in real time — green means balanced.
📋 Scenarios
Real-world scenario prompts mirroring the 20 interview demos. Each scenario describes a situation and asks what you would do — ideal for verbal practice and self-testing.
⭐ Assistant Controller Panel
Elevated tools for an Assistant Controller role — includes month-end close checklist, KPI dashboard, risk register, and internal controls matrix. Perfect for showcasing leadership-level readiness.
🚀 Getting Started
  • 1
    Click the 🎓 STUDY MODE tab in the top navigation bar to enter this panel.
  • 2
    Choose a study mode from the left sidebar — start with Flashcards for a broad review, then move to Quiz Mode.
  • 3
    In Flashcards: click any card to flip it and reveal the answer. Use the ← PREV / NEXT → buttons to move between cards.
  • 4
    In Quiz Mode: click an answer option. It will immediately show green (correct) or red (wrong), and the explanation panel expands below.
  • 5
    In JE Builder: fill in the GL Account, Description, Debit, and Credit columns. Watch the balance checker — it must read BALANCED before posting.
  • 6
    In Scenarios: read each prompt and practice your verbal answer out loud or type notes in the response field.
  • 7
    Use the ⭐ Assistant Controller Panel to explore higher-level tools — complete the month-end checklist and review the risk register.
  • 8
    Your Study Progress in the left sidebar updates automatically as you complete each mode.
💡 Pro Tips
⚡
Run through all 20 flashcards before your interview session. They map 1-to-1 with each demo step.
🎯
In Quiz Mode, don't just check the answer — read the full explanation every time, even when you're right.
📒
Memorize the GL account numbers (e.g. 6140 = Legal, 2150 = Accrued Liabilities). Interviewers notice when you cite them without looking.
✏️
Practice the JE Builder with the exact $7,500 legal accrual from Demo 11. That specific entry appears in 3 different demos.
⭐
If applying for an Assistant Controller role, spend extra time in the AC Panel — it shows strategic awareness beyond the Staff Accountant baseline.
🔁
After studying, switch back to the 20 Demo Playbook tab and run all demos in AUTO-RUN mode to simulate the real assessment flow.

🔒 INTERNAL CONTROLS MATRIX
⚖️
Segregation of Duties
No single user approves + releases payments. $10K threshold enforced in FinOps UI.
📝
Audit Trail Logging
All actions timestamped, IP-logged, immutable. Exportable for compliance review.
🔁
3-Way Match
PO → Invoice → Receipt verified before AP release. Auto-flags discrepancies.
🏦
Bank Reconciliation
Monthly auto-match cycle. Unreconciled items escalated at >$0.01 variance.
📊
Budget-to-Actual Review
Variance alerts trigger at >5% deviation. Drill-down to source transaction level.
🔐
Period Lock Controls
Prior periods locked post-close. Retroactive changes require Controller sign-off and audit note.

Risk Communication
"I maintain a live risk register and proactively surface items to the Controller — not after period-end but mid-month, so there's time to investigate and resolve before the close window."
Team Leadership
"I review and approve Staff Accountant journal entries before they hit the GL. I coach on the SoD policy and controls framework so the team understands the why, not just the rules."
System Mastery
"I use the FinOps platform across all three layers — the UI for KPI visibility, Operations for transaction-level investigation, and Accounting for GL entries, trial balance, and reporting. I bridge all three in real time."
