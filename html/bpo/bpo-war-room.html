► ENGINE PIPELINE

E01 Document Intelligence — extracts all key facts, parties, amounts, deadlines
E02 Issue Analysis — identifies root causes, risk flags, denial patterns
E03 Financial Impact — quantifies dollar exposure, recovery probability
E04 Compliance & Legal — audits regulatory obligations, filing deadlines
E05 Recovery Plan — numbered step-by-step actions for each remediation app
E06 App Dispatch — routes defects to specific remediation tools with field-level instructions

► REMEDIATION TOOL PANEL

After engines complete, click any remediation tile (Financial, Audit Ops, Legal, Compliance, Zero-Trust, FinOps Accounting, FinOps Ops) to open the tool in the panel below. The app receives defect context from Engine 06.

► ESCALATION

After analysis, escalate to the BPO Strategist. The Strategist synthesizes all 6 engine outputs into a unified action plan and executive brief.
