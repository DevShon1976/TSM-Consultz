Daily Operations Workflow
How to run your day — start to finish
Follow this sequence every operating day. Each phase uses a specific app. Don't skip the morning review — it sets your AI context for the day.
1
Morning Command Review
hub-index.html → Executive Portal Live
10 MIN ▶
  1. 1Open Command Hub (/html/hub-index.html) — this is your TSM Hub dashboard showing all apps.
  2. 2Launch Executive Portal Live — review any flagged items, active client work, or pending tasks from yesterday.
  3. 3Check Enterprise WIP Dashboard — see what's in progress across all service lines.
  4. 4Open Strategist Command Center — note any escalation rules triggered overnight.
⚡ MORNING CONTEXT PROMPTS
→Summarize the top 3 priority actions I should take today based on current BPO service demand trends in healthcare billing and insurance
→What are the most common issues BPO healthcare clients face on Monday mornings and how should I prepare?
2
Active Client Work
Sector-specific node → BPO Command Center
CORE HOURS ▶
  1. 1Navigate to the client's sector app (e.g., healthcare-bpo.html for HC clients, insurance-bpo.html for insurance).
  2. 2Use the intake fields to load client context — paste in the issue, claim, document, or request.
  3. 3Run the appropriate AI workflow (the node system prompt is pre-loaded for each vertical).
  4. 4Export, copy, or document the AI output for delivery to the client.
  5. 5Log completed work in BPO Internal Operations (bpo-internal1.html).
3
Prospecting & Sales
Demo Decks → Sales Scripts → Suite Builder
1-2 HRS ▶
  1. 1Use sales-scripts.html to generate personalized outreach scripts for the prospect's vertical.
  2. 2Pull the matching demo deck (e.g., tsm-hc-demo-trail.html for healthcare prospects).
  3. 3Use Suite Builder to customize a package proposal for the specific prospect.
  4. 4Prep ROI numbers using Financial Scenarios or the BPO ROI tool.
⚡ SALES PROMPTS
→Write a cold outreach script targeting a healthcare billing manager at a 50-physician group practice, focusing on denial rate reduction
→What ROI can a mid-size medical group expect by replacing their BPO billing vendor with TSM? Give me specific numbers.
→Create a 3-minute elevator pitch for TSM BPO services vs a traditional offshore BPO for insurance eligibility verification
4
EOD Wrap + Training
WIP Dashboard → Training Apps
30 MIN ▶
  1. 1Update WIP Dashboard with completed work and any items carrying over.
  2. 2Spend 20-30 min in a training app (CRC Practice Lab, CRCR Scenarios, or AHIP Prep) to sharpen domain knowledge.
  3. 3Note any new prompts or workflows that worked well — save them to your prompt library.

Healthcare BPO Execution — Step by Step
▶

The exact language that gets the best outputs
HC
Healthcare Revenue Cycle Prompts
▶
BILLING & DENIALS
→This claim was denied [denial code]. The CPT code is [code], payer is [payer], DOS [date]. Identify the root cause and provide a step-by-step appeal strategy with specific letter language.
→Review this EOB and identify all billing errors, underpayments, and opportunities for adjustment. Prioritize by dollar amount.
→Write a prior authorization narrative for [procedure/CPT code] for a [diagnosis] patient. Payer is [payer]. Include medical necessity language aligned with their coverage criteria.
→Our collection rate for [payer] dropped from 94% to 87% this quarter. Analyze likely causes and give me a 30-day remediation plan.
COMPLIANCE
→Conduct a HIPAA breach risk assessment for this incident: [describe incident]. Identify whether notification is required under the 4-factor test and draft the appropriate response.
→Review this [policy type] policy against current [CMS/HIPAA/OSHA] requirements. Flag gaps and suggest compliant replacement language.

→Verify benefits for member [ID], plan [plan name], for service [service type]. Summarize deductible, copay, OOP max, and any applicable exclusions or limitations.

→Analyze this payer contract's timely filing requirements, audit provisions, and recoupment rights. Flag any terms that put us at financial risk.

BPO Battle Cards
How to win against traditional BPO providers

"You're too small / new" Big BPO = safer We run on the same AI infrastructure as Fortune 500 companies. Our size means you get direct access to the strategist layer — not a ticket queue and an offshore rep.

"HIPAA concerns with AI" AI isn't safe for PHI We operate under BAA frameworks. Our platform architecture is built for healthcare compliance. Traditional BPOs with offshore staff have far more exposure than our closed-loop AI system.
