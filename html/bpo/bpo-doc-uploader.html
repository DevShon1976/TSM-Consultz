► WHAT IS THE BPO DOC UPLOADER?

The BPO Document Intelligence Uploader is Stage 01 of TSM's 4-stage BPO workflow chain. Upload or paste any business document — denial, contract, invoice, claim, compliance notice, or financial report — and route it to the 6-engine BPO War Room for full AI analysis.

► 4-STAGE CHAIN

Stage 01 · Uploader → Classify and load your document
Stage 02 · War Room → 6-engine AI analysis pipeline
Stage 03 · Strategist → Synthesis and action plan
Stage 04 · Executive Portal → Executive brief and KPI dashboard

► QUICK START

1. Select your sector (Healthcare, FinOps, Construction, etc.)
2. Select document type
3. Upload file OR paste text OR click a sample chip
4. Click ⚡ ANALYZE IN BPO WAR ROOM

PRO TIPS
▸ Use sample chips to demo the full chain without a real document
▸ Sector + Doc Type selection primes the War Room engine prompts for better output
▸ All data relays via sessionStorage — no server uploads required
▸ The chain remembers your context through all 4 stages
