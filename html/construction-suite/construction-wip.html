AZ MECHANIC'S LIEN RULES · CRITICAL DATES
AZ LAW
Preliminary 20-Day Notice: Subs and suppliers must serve owner/GC within 20 days of first furnishing labor/materials. Missing this ELIMINATES lien rights. Verify all subs filed before starting work.
AZ LAW
Lien Filing Deadline: 120 days from substantial completion OR last work. GC has 120 days; subs have 120 days from their last work. Foreclosure must follow within 6 months.
AZ LAW
Conditional vs Unconditional: Conditional waiver releases lien rights conditioned on payment clearing. Unconditional = absolute release. NEVER accept unconditional before payment clears bank.
