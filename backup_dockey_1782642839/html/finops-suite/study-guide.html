HOW TO · HC PRACTICE HUB

Step-by-Step: How to Run a CRCR Mission

Follow these 6 steps every session to build exam confidence through real operational execution.
STEP 1
Pick a Domain
Click CRCR · RCM tab → choose Intake, Coding, Claims, Denials, Payments, or Financial
STEP 2
Click PRACTICE HERE
Opens the Mission Panel with numbered objectives and node launch links
STEP 3
View Objectives
Right panel shows your mission — risk score, objectives, and which HC Node to use
STEP 4
Click Objective
Navigates to the HC Command Hub — correct node highlighted with instructions
STEP 5
Complete the Task
Review the node — read TOP ISSUE and WHY IT MATTERS — use OPEN NODE for deep workflow
STEP 6
Mark Complete
Click green banner → score updates → return to practice → risk score drops
CRCR EXAM CONNECTION: Every objective maps to a real CRCR exam competency. The denial code you practice resolving, the auth you verify, the ERA you reconcile — these are the exact scenarios tested on the CRCR exam. The platform trains you to execute, not just memorize.
APP 2 · FINANCIAL OPERATIONS

FinOps Suite — Complete Feature Map

The FinOps Suite is your staff accountant training platform and financial operations simulator. Built around 20 live demos with real PoC data.
1

20 Demo Playbook

4 categories × 5 demos each: Daily Workflow, Reconciliation, General Ledger, Compliance. Each demo auto-runs with real PoC data. Candidates narrate and execute — proving mastery to interviewers or managers.

20 DEMO PLAYBOOK TAB
2

POC Data — Live Datasets

Real demo data: invoice batch (INV-2026-001 through 004, $23K), bank feed (TXN-BK-9901/9902/9905), fixed assets ($15K server + $12K insurance), journal entries (JE with auto-reverse). All injectable into the UI.

POC DATA TAB
3

Live Ledger

Real-time general ledger view with trial balance, account balances, and journal entry history. Used in Demo 9 (sub-ledger to GL tie-out) and Demo 11 (JE posting and review).

LIVE LEDGER TAB
4

Reconciliation

Bank reconciliation engine with auto-match, manual match, and variance resolution. The $15 bank fee split (Demo 8) and $48K variance investigation (FinOps Scenario 3) are practiced here.

RECONCILIATION TAB
5

Score Card

Live assessment scoring across all 20 demos. Tracks completion rate, accuracy, and category performance. Used for interview assessments — gives interviewers an objective readiness score.

SCORE CARD TAB
6

POC Document Portal — Generate Professional Documents

Every scenario in both apps has a corresponding document output. The POC Portal generates state-of-the-art professional documents for managers, CFOs, and compliance officers.

🏥 Healthcare Documents

  • Formal denial appeal letters (CO-29, CO-197, CO-4)
  • Prior auth escalation memos for payer relations
  • CFO briefings on denial rate analysis and recovery plans
  • Compliance audit summaries (HIPAA, OIG, CMS)
  • Office manager operations reports
  • HRSA grant compliance reports
  • Coding audit findings and corrective action plans
  • Executive briefings for leadership meetings

📊 Financial Documents

  • CFO variance memos (GL budget vs actual)
  • AP aging analysis with payment priority recommendations
  • Bank reconciliation exception reports
  • Vendor escalation letters for SLA breaches
  • Board-ready financial summaries
  • Payroll compliance audit reports
  • Month-end close packages
  • Underpayment appeal packages for payer recovery

📋 When to Use the POC Portal

Use it at the end of every scenario — after completing the operational work, generate the document that would accompany the real-world action. This is what CFOs and managers actually need.
