⚡ HOW TO USE — INSURANCE WAR ROOM

STEP 1 — LOAD YOUR DOCUMENT

  • Drop a claims document, EOB, policy filing, underwriting report, or compliance notice into the intake zone — or paste raw text.
  • Use a sample document to demo the pipeline instantly.

STEP 2 — CONFIGURE API KEY

  • Enter your Groq API key and hit Test. Green dot = connected. Saved to localStorage.

STEP 3 — RUN THE ENGINE PIPELINE

  • E1 · DOCUMENT INTEL — Extracts policy numbers, claim IDs, denial codes, coverage types, and dates.
  • E2 · COVERAGE ANALYST — Maps denial to policy language, identifies coverage gaps and exclusions.
  • E3 · FINANCIAL IMPACT — Quantifies dollar exposure, reserve requirements, and subrogation potential.
  • E4 · RECOVERY STRATEGY — Builds appeal plan, re-submission checklist, and regulatory escalation path.
  • E5 · NAVIGATOR — Routes tasks to Claims Adjuster, Underwriter, Compliance Officer, or Carrier Contact.

STEP 4 — NARRATED TOUR

  • Hit the 🎙 SPEAK button to activate ResponsiveVoice narration of engine output.
  • Use the App Dispatch panel to route to the recommended Insurance suite app.
